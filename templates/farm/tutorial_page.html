How FarmFlow Works

A complete guide to managing your farm efficiently with FarmFlow. Follow these simple steps to get started.

1

Create Your Account

Start your journey by creating a free FarmFlow account. Simply click "Get Started" and fill in your basic information including username, email, and password. Once registered, you'll get instant access to your personalized dashboard.

  • Quick 2-minute registration
  • 14-day free trial with full access
  • No credit card required
  • Instant dashboard access

Registration Demo

2

Explore Your Dashboard

Your dashboard is the command center for your farm. Get real-time insights, track key metrics, and access all features from one intuitive interface. View stats for crops, livestock, finances, and tasks at a glance.

  • Personalized dashboard view
  • Real-time data and analytics
  • Quick action shortcuts
  • Mobile-responsive design

Dashboard Overview

3

Add & Manage Crops

Track your crops from planting to harvest. Record crop types, planting dates, expected yields, and monitor growth stages. Upload photos to document progress and maintain detailed records.

  • Track multiple crop varieties
  • Monitor planting and harvest dates
  • Record yield and quality data
  • Photo documentation

Crop Management

4

Track Your Livestock

Maintain comprehensive records for all your animals. Track health status, breeding dates, vaccinations, and production records. Receive alerts for important dates and health checks.

  • Individual animal profiles
  • Health and vaccination tracking
  • Breeding history management
  • Production records

Livestock Management

5

Manage Your Finances

Keep track of all farm income and expenses. Generate financial reports, monitor profitability, and make data-driven decisions. Upload receipts and categorize transactions easily.

  • Track income and expenses
  • Automatic profit calculations
  • Receipt upload and storage
  • Financial reports and analytics

Financial Tracking

6

Organize Tasks & Activities

Never miss important farm activities. Create tasks, set priorities, assign to team members, and track completion. Get notifications for upcoming deadlines and overdue tasks.

  • Create and assign tasks
  • Set due dates and priorities
  • Track task completion
  • Activity logging and history
